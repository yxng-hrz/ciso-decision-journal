# CISO Decision Journal

## Role Context
- Position: Chief Information Security Officer
- Reporting: CIO & CEO
- Authority: Risk veto on critical decisions

---

## Decision 1 – Company-wide MFA
**Context:** Increase in phishing attacks.

**Options:**
1. MFA for admins only
2. MFA for all users

**Decision:** Option 2

**Rationale:**
- Humans are primary attack vector
- High risk reduction
- Acceptable usability impact

**Accepted Risk:** MFA fatigue attacks

---

## Decision 2 – Product vs Security
**Context:** Commercial pressure to release features.

**Decision:** Delay release by 2 weeks for security review.

**Trade-off:** Short-term revenue pressure vs long-term trust.

---

## Decision 3 – Minor Data Leak
**Context:** Unauthorized data export detected.

**Actions:**
- Investigation
- Containment
- Legal assessment

**CNIL Notification:** Not required

---

## Decision 4 – Security Budget Allocation

| Area | Amount |
|----|-------|

| Awareness | €10k |

---

## Decision 5 – ISO 27001 Request
**Decision:** ISO-aligned program without certification.

**Justification:** Cost, realism, transparency.
