# CISO Decision Journal

## Decision 1 – Company-wide MFA
Humans are the primary attack vector.
MFA deployed for all internal users.

## Decision 2 – Security vs Product Delivery
Feature release delayed by two weeks for security review.

## Decision 3 – Refusal of Unrealistic Security Guarantees
A major deal was declined to avoid legal and reputational exposure.

## Decision 4 – Budget Allocation Without SIEM
Preventive controls prioritized.
Risk formally accepted and documented.

## Decision 5 – Incident Disclosure Strategy
Transparency balanced with legal and reputational considerations.
