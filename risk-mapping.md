# Enterprise Risk Mapping

## Critical Assets
| Asset | Description | Impact |
|------|------------|--------|
| Customer Data | Personal & contractual data | Critical |
| SaaS Platform | Core revenue service | Critical |
| Cloud Infrastructure | Compute, IAM, network | High |
| Brand Reputation | Customer trust | High |
| Key Personnel | DevOps, senior engineers | Medium |

## Threat Landscape
- Ransomware
- Account compromise (phishing)
- Third-party breach
- Human error
- Cloud outage

## Risk Scenarios
### R1 – Ransomware on production
Likelihood: Medium | Impact: Very High | Risk: Critical

### R2 – Data breach via compromised account
Likelihood: High | Impact: High | Risk: Critical

### R3 – Prolonged service outage
Likelihood: Medium | Impact: High | Risk: High

### R4 – Regulatory non-compliance (GDPR/NIS2)
Likelihood: Medium | Impact: Medium/High | Risk: High

## Risk Strategy
Risks are reduced where cost-effective.
Residual risks are explicitly accepted at executive level.
